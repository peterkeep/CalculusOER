Theorem 1.3.6. Limits of Two Basic Functions.

Let \(a\) be some real number.
  1. Limit of a Constant Function: If \(k\) is some real number constant, then:
    \begin{equation*} \lim_{x\to a} k = k \end{equation*}
  2. Limit of the Identity Function:
    \begin{equation*} \lim_{x\to a} x = a \end{equation*}
