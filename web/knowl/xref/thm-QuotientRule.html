Theorem 2.4.5. Quotient Rule.

If \(u(x)\) and \(v(x)\) are differentiable at \(x\) and \(f(x) = \dfrac{u(x)}{v(x)}\) then:
\begin{equation*} f'(x) = \dfrac{v(x)u'(x) - u(x)v'(x)}{(v(x))^2}\text{.} \end{equation*}
