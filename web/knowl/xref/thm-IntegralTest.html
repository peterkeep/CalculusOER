Theorem 8.4.2. Integral Test.

If \(\sum_{k=0}^\infty a_k\) is an infinite series with \(a_k\gt 0\) for all \(k\geq 0\) and \(f(x)\) is a continuous and decreasing function with \(f(k) = a_k\) for all \(k\geq 0\text{,}\) then we can compare the behaviors of \(\sum_{k=0}^\infty a_k\) and \(\int_{x=0}^\infty f(x)\;dx\text{:}\) the integral and the series are guaranteed to either both diverge or both converge.
