Definition 3.1.4. Increasing and Decreasing on an Interval.

A function \(f(x)\) is increasing on the interval \((a,b)\) if, for every pair of \(x\)-values \(x_1\) and \(x_2\) (with \(x_1 \lt x_2\)), \(f(x_1)\ \lt f(x_2)\text{.}\)
