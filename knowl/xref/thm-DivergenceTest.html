Theorem 8.3.3. Divergence Test.

For an infinite series \(\sum_{k=0}^\infty a_k\text{,}\) if the infinite series converges then
\begin{equation*} \lim_{k\to\infty} a_k = 0\text{.} \end{equation*}
This is equivalent to saying that if
\begin{equation*} \lim_{k\to\infty} a_k\neq 0 \end{equation*}
then the infinite series \(\sum_{k=0}^\infty a_k\) diverges.
