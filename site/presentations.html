Interests

I've had the chance to present and host workshops at a number of different places. Much of what I present about is related to alternative grading and assessments, open educational resources, or the specific lens through which I teach calculus. I really enjoy not just sharing my perspective on these different topics, but starting a dialogue in the room with participants.

I'm always interested in connecting with faculty and modeling the safe and open spaces I create in my own classrooms. If you're interested in having me work with your department or colleagues around topics like alternative grading, open educational resources, or approaches to teaching calculus, please reach out!
